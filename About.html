About

Fitness Club is the best CrossFit website template that works fantastically well for your action and sports facility. Of course, if you like the look, you can also use this for gyms and fitnesses, heck, even personal trainers. The tool is versatile and highly adaptive, as well as welcomes any customization tweak you would like to perform. Moreover, Fitness Gym features a full-screen slider, CTA buttons, pricing tables, scrolling animations, and class schedule/timetable. You can also activate blog pages and BMI calculator and increase user experience. This is a Bootstrap 5 website template, so you know it follows all the latest trends. In other words, your CrossFit website will run smoothly on modern devices and platforms.
